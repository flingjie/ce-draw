# ER Diagram Prompt

## Reasoning Rules

- Maximum 6 entities per diagram
- Show primary keys, foreign keys, relationships
- Use crow's foot notation for cardinality
- Group related entities visually

## Styling Constraints

- Maximum 5 colors
- Leave 120px margin
- Consistent entity widths
- Hand-drawn roughness: 1
